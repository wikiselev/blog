About


I am the head of Cellular Genetics Informatics team at the Wellcome Sanger Institute. My team provides efficient access to cutting-edge analysis methods for Cellular Genetics department, which leads a number of world-class research initiatives including the Human Induced Pluripotent Stem Cell Initiative (HIPSCI) and Human Cell Atlas (HCA). Our team’s focus is the development and operation of workflows, tools and infrastructure for data analysis that support the department’s research goals.
